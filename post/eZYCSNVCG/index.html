Abstract:

Peptide is rich in nutrition and has a variety of physiological activities, which can protect the liver from damage from the following aspects:

Chronic active hepatitis

The reason why acute viral hepatitis turns into chronic active hepatitis is related to the disorder of immune function. The cellular and humoral immune responses of such patients are weak, and the virus in the cells cannot be completely removed, but it is released to the outside of the cells, and the antibody cannot completely prevent it from entering the uninfected hepatocytes, thus causing the continuous infection of the virus. In addition, due to the dysfunction of immune function and the defect of T-lymphocyte function, the HBsAg in hepatocytes enters the blood stream, so most chronic hepatitis B patients are tested positive for HBsAg.

The effect of Sanjiu protein peptide on chronic hepatitis

\1. Peptide contains immune regulatory factors, which can effectively regulate the immune function of the body, enhance the humoral immune function of cells, improve the serum c-globulin, and completely eliminate the intracellular hepatitis virus.

\2. Peptide supplement antibody and complement, effectively prevent the virus from entering the normal hepatocyte, prevent the continuous infection of hepatitis virus.

\3. Repair the damaged hepatocytes, improve the liver function and relieve the symptoms of digestive tract.

Early liver cirrhosis, fatty liver, alcoholic liver disease, drug liver disease and other liver disease patients generally have low immune function, which directly results in the decline of the body's ability to recognize and inhibit the virus. The most obvious manifestation is that the virus in the body is difficult to completely eliminate, and the disease is easy to recur.

The effect of peptide on liver diseases

\1. Peptide has the function of protecting, repairing and activating hepatocytes, making the hardened liver gradually return to normal and maintaining the normal physiological function of liver.

\2. Peptide can improve the catabolism of hepatocytes, clear the fat in liver, and have a better effect on fatty liver.

\3. Peptide can also enhance the function of alcohol dehydrogenase and oxygenase, accelerate the decomposition of alcohol into carbon dioxide and water (CO2 ↑ and H2O ↓), so it has the function of alcohol detoxification.

\4. Peptide can repair, activate and promote liver cells, so it can restore normal function of liver damaged by drugs.
