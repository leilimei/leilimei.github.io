How to replenish collagen after 30 years old

2020-04-26

1、Collagen supplement after 30 years old

2、Look at the formula before choosing collagen

3、Eating animal skin is not good for collagen supplement

Summary:

Over the age of 30, our own collagen production will decrease with age. The loss of collagen results in the breakage of collagen peptide bond and elastic net supporting the skin, the destruction of its spiral net structure, the oxidation, atrophy and collapse of skin tissue, and the aging phenomenon of dryness, wrinkles, relaxation and inelasticity will occur. Therefore, to delay aging, collagen must be supplemented.

1、Collagen supplement after 30 years old

Skin expert Li Guangrui introduced that in the dermis of human skin, 75% of the ingredients are collagen. When we are young, the body will produce a lot of collagen, but after 30 years old, its production will decrease with the increase of age. Therefore, generally speaking, women over 30 years old need to supplement collagen to deal with skin dryness, fine lines, relaxation and other skin problems caused by collagen loss.

In addition, women in the following stages also need to pay attention to collagen supplement: UV active summer; new mother stage, childbearing and lactation will consume a lot of collagen in the human body; During diet and weight loss, the collagen absorbed from food is less than before. In fact, collagen supplement will not only not increase fat, but also help speed up fat metabolism and make muscles more compact.

2、Look at the formula before choosing collagen

In recent years, collagen is very hot. There are many kinds of collagen products on the market, including many mixed products. What should we pay attention to when choosing collagen?

Experts remind that to choose collagen products, we should first recognize the formula, and try to avoid choosing collagen products without any compound. Such products do not contain VC, polyphenols and other antioxidants, so the absorption effect will be discounted.

Secondly, we should pay attention to identify whether there are unsuitable ingredients in the formula, such as natural estrogens such as soybean extract, which may increase the risk of breast lobular hyperplasia and uterine fibroids for young women with high estrogen level.

Finally, we should pay more attention to whether the formula of collagen products has strong pertinence. Any nutritional ingredients beneficial to human body need to reach a certain dose to play a role. The daily loss of collagen in human body is about 2000mg-3000mg. If you want to buy collagen products for beauty, you'd better choose products with collagen content of more than 5000mg.

3、Eating animal skin is not good for collagen supplement

People have always said that eating pigskin, chicken skin and other beauty. However, experts point out that it is wrong to think that as long as you eat more animal skin, you can supplement collagen for beauty.

In addition, in order to fight against aging, women need to do a good job in this subject - reduce the destruction and loss of collagen. First of all, we should pay attention to sun protection, because the ultraviolet light in the sun has a destructive effect on the collagen of the skin. We should also take more foods containing antioxidants. Vitamin C not only plays a role in promoting the synthesis of collagen, but also is a good antioxidant. It is recommended to eat more vegetables and fruits rich in vitamin C, such as carrots, tomatoes, kiwifruit and grapes.
