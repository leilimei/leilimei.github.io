Treatment of symptoms and root causes, world traditional medicine day, understand?

2020-03-30

Shennong has been enjoying the story of tasting hundreds of herbs for a long time. He personally tasted hundreds of herbs, developed the treatment of diseases with herbs, invented the pharmaceutical industry, laid the foundation of Chinese traditional medicine, and created the Chinese traditional medicine culture.

Chinese traditional medicine: Traditional Chinese medicine, ethnic medicine and other folk medicine. "Medicine" has a history of thousands of years. Even in the era of Western medicine, it has not been eliminated. Nowadays, it pays more attention to the combination of Chinese and Western medicine, and strives for indicators to cure the root cause.

In 1991, the State Administration of traditional Chinese medicine and the World Health Organization jointly held the international traditional medicine conference in Beijing. At the international traditional medicine conference held in Beijing in December of the same year, the representatives of 42 countries and regions unanimously decided to set the opening day of the conference as the world traditional medicine day every year, that is, the day every year, and wrote it into the Beijing Declaration.

Declaration of material cultural heritage

With the side effects of chemicals, drug-induced diseases, high cost and other issues, traditional Chinese medicine has become one of the main representatives of traditional medicine in the world, and widely used in Southeast Asia, Japan, South Korea and other countries, as well as the United States, Europe and other western developed countries gradually pay attention to traditional Chinese medicine.

Since 2003, in order to avoid the cultural heritage of traditional Chinese medicine being declared by other countries in the first place, the State Administration of traditional Chinese medicine began to prepare the application of traditional Chinese medicine for the world cultural heritage, and maintained communication with UNESCO to declare traditional Chinese medicine as the world intangible cultural heritage as a whole.

Traditional Chinese medicine can become the representative of traditional medicine, because it is based on the Han culture, with Huangdi Neijing, Shennong bencaojing, treatise on febrile diseases, synopsis of the golden chamber as the basic classic traditional medicine, which has long been the mainstream medicine in Chinese society.

Human health needs traditional medicine, and the traditional medicine of every country in the world is a rare wealth of the international pharmaceutical industry.

Modern medicine, as the counterpart of traditional medicine, also plays an extremely important role in human health. Just like the combination of Chinese and Western medicine, traditional Chinese medicine pursues the origin, pays attention to conditioning, and Western medicine pays attention to quick results. The combination of the two will achieve better results.
