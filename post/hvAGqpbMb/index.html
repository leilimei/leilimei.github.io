"Five views" teaches you to recognize good peptides

Zou Yuandong, a famous expert in enzymatic peptide, taught us some simple and easy tips for peptide recognition

Peptide recognition depends on the size of its molecular weight segment. The compound of 2-9 amino acid chains connected by peptide bond is called small peptide. It has the highest value in all peptides, has strong activity and diversity, has important biological functions, can give full play to human physiological functions and enhance human physiological activities. It is an important physiological regulator. Therefore, when people choose peptide products, they should look at its molecular weight segment. Those with molecular weight segment below 1000 belong to small peptide. The peptides with molecular weight range of 1000-5000 belong to large peptides, which have different value compared with small peptides. Small peptides are more expensive than gold. But macropeptides are cheap.

Peptide recognition depends on its peptide content. The content of some peptide mixtures is very low, but the content of small peptide decomposed by protein isolate is particularly high, its energy and function are several times higher than the former.
