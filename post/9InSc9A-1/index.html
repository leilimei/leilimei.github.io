What effect does small molecule peptide have to the eye?

In the whole vision system, cornea is one of the most important structures, in which collagen peptide fibers are arranged in a regular line. This structure not only allows light to pass through smoothly, but also because of the special arrangement of collagen peptide fibers, cornea can be more transparent, brighter and clearer.

However, with the loss of collagen in the cornea, the arrangement of collagen fibers in the cornea was disturbed, and the vision began to decline, blur, even lead to cataract and other eye diseases.

The most common eye diseases are keratitis, conjunctivitis, astigmatism, myopia, amblyopia, glaucoma, trachoma, vitreous opacity, cataract, ametropia, wheat swelling, red eye disease, retinitis, etc.

Causes of eye diseases

There are many causes of ophthalmopathy, mainly due to improper use of the eye and malnutrition and other aspects of the body.

Because of long-term eyesight fatigue, aging, the softness of the eyeball becomes poor, and the elasticity of the lens decreases.

For long-term close eye, the lens axis cannot be recovered after stretching, or the telescope axis is limited, and the diopter cannot be adjusted freely, so that the focus of light deviates from the retina, resulting in blurred imaging, resulting in myopia and presbyopia.

The effect of small molecular peptide on eyes

The main components in the lens of the eye are collagen and a large number of peptides, namely neuropeptide, enkephalin, etc.

Small molecular peptide can improve the transparency of the vitreous and cornea, and keep the vitreous and cornea transparent. When the content of collagen peptide in the eyeball decreases, the fiber ages and collapses, the cornea will become turbid and cataract and other eye diseases will begin to occur.

Therefore, the supplement of small molecular peptide plays an important role in improving the health and sensitivity of retina and optic nerve. General eye diseases, as long as a certain dose of small molecular peptide is continuously supplemented, can get a good recovery for eye diseases.
