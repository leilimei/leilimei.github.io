1、 What is a small peptide?

The amino group of one amino acid and the carboxyl group of another amino acid can be condensed into peptide, and the amide group formed is called peptide bond in protein chemistry. Amino acids are the smallest molecules. Proteins are composed of two or more amino acids that dehydrate and condense to form several peptide bonds to form a peptide. Multiple peptides are folded to form a protein molecule.

Peptide is the basic substance of human body. It is a compound with two or more amino acids connected by peptide bond. It plays an important physiological role in human body and plays a physiological function. All kinds of cell functions and all life behaviors, such as growth, development, reproduction, metabolism, action, etc., in human body must be embodied by peptide. Peptide is the key substance to determine the quality of human life.

Generally speaking, the function of peptide to human body is to inhibit, activate, promote and repair.
