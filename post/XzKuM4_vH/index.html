Nutritional effects of bioactive peptides on pregnant women

2020-03-09

Pregnant women are a kind of people who need special nutrition. Slight neglect may cause irreparable harm. As a nutritional supplement, bioactive peptides can not only supplement nutrition, but also produce unexpected functions for the body.

Peptide and nutrition of pregnant women

Pregnancy is a complex physiological process. During pregnancy, pregnant women need to make a series of physiological adjustments to adapt to the growth and development of the fetus in the body, absorb maternal nutrition and excrete waste. Nutrition during pregnancy has an important influence on the growth and development of offspring. Severe malnutrition of pregnant mothers can cause abortion, premature delivery, physical growth of fetus and brain development retardation. And the mother's resistance is weak during pregnancy. After bacterial infection, it is easy to hinder the development of the fetus, resulting in congenital malformation of the fetus.

Changes of nutritional metabolism during pregnancy

The demand for total energy and protein, lipid, carbohydrate, mineral and vitamin increased during pregnancy.

Calcium intake should be increased during pregnancy to ensure the demand of mother's bone calcium and meet the demand of fetus for calcium;

Iron deficiency anemia is a common nutritional problem. There is also a lot of evidence that iron deficiency in early pregnancy is associated with premature delivery and low birth

Body weight is related to iron deficiency anemia, but it is related to insufficient weight gain during pregnancy;

The intake of zinc and zinc is enough to promote the growth and development of fetus and prevent congenital malformation.

Peptide and cesarean section

In recent years, the cesarean section rate of pregnant women has increased in a straight line, some hospitals as high as 60% - 80%. But cesarean section is a kind of invasive injury to the parturient. It can make the wound heal slowly, inflame and increase the risk of infection. With the liberalization of the two child policy, poor wound healing also has an impact on the second pregnancy, which can increase the risk of placenta previa. Placenta previa is easy to cause premature delivery, and fetal distress may occur due to maternal shock. Severe hypoxia of the fetus may lead to fetal death,

It can also die of poor living ability of premature infants. So placenta previa is very dangerous.

Therefore, taking small molecule bioactive peptide after childbirth can provide rich nutrition for the body, accelerate the wound healing, make the wound surface smooth without scab, increase the skin's anti pulling force, reduce the risk of placenta previa in the second pregnancy, and make the maternal body have a better improvement.
