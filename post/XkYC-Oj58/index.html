Abstract:

The order of human body composition is: system, organ, tissue and cell. Cells are the smallest and most important living unit of our body. Once there is a problem with cells, there will be problems with tissues, organs and systems. All the food we eat in our life is to provide our cells with comprehensive and sufficient nutrition. Then because of the influence of various factors, our cells can not get the proper nutrition, and people will get sick.

The effective way to strengthen the body is to repair cells, improve cells and activate cells. It is suggested to supplement cellular nutrients: small molecule active peptide.

Small molecule active peptide will not be hydrolyzed by various enzymes and acid-base substances of human body after being taken by human body.   1. Fast absorption without waiting

The small molecule active peptide is 70% faster than amino acid. It can pass through human mouth and stomach quickly, and enter into tissues in urgent need directly through small intestine.

  1. No defect, complete absorption

Small molecule active peptide is absorbed and utilized by human body in complete form, so it can give full play to the function of peptide.

  1. No loss, efficient absorption

The small molecule active peptide has the characteristics of high-efficiency absorption by the human body. After absorption, there will be no excreta, and it is all absorbed and utilized by the human body

  1. Biological activity, active absorption

For those who cannot absorb nutrients due to digestive system defects, obstacles and damages, small molecule active peptide has the characteristics of active absorption or forced absorption by human body. This is of great significance to those who are weak in body, poor in digestion and nutrition.
