What is chronic disease?

Chronic disease - chronic disease refers to the general term of diseases that do not constitute infection and have long-term accumulation to form disease morphological damage. Chronic diseases will cause great harm. If the prevention and treatment is not enough, it will cause economic, life and other hazards.

Common chronic diseases include diabetes, cancer, chronic liver disease, chronic kidney disease, chronic gastrointestinal disease, cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases, etc.

The harm of these chronic diseases is mainly the damage of brain, heart, kidney and other important organs, which is easy to cause disability, affect the ability to work and quality of life, and the medical cost is extremely expensive, which increases the economic burden of society and family.

The "savior" of chronic diseases - nutrition intervention!

For chronic diseases, especially diet related chronic diseases, the causes are mostly due to poor dietary patterns and lifestyle. To solve these chronic diseases, the most appropriate way is to start from the causes, through the use of a reasonable diet mode, to establish a correct way of life to solve. Nutrition intervention is to help correct the unreasonable diet or the deficiency of some nutrients intake through the reasonable collocation of food raw materials or food ingredients. At the same time, nutrition intervention food has no side effects of drugs, which is more suitable for the treatment of chronic diseases.

The importance of nutrition intervention "peptide"

Due to the influence of various factors such as environment and lifestyle, the content of peptide in our body is very small, and the metabolism of cells needs peptide, but our body can not degrade enough peptide. In the long run, the origin of our life, cells, will continue to be damaged, and then cause various chronic diseases.

Scientists have found that oligopeptides can prevent and treat the occurrence and development of a variety of chronic diseases through anti-oxidation stress, improving immunity and anti-inflammatory response. Oligopeptides, as a special nutrient in nutritional support and treatment, have high clinical application value.
