Shengnuo bio participation in CPHI India 2018

2020-03-24

In the middle of December 2018, under the leadership of Sichuan business development association, Chengdu sannuobi was invited to attend the 2018 international pharmaceutical raw materials India exhibition, and at the exhibition, the company's business profile and main products, polypeptides and other drugs were displayed in detail for 3 days.

More than 1100 companies from more than 100 countries and regions, including China, France, Germany, Italy, the United Kingdom, the United States, Japan and Singapore, participated in the exhibition, including nearly 380 companies from India.


Figure 1: sinobio's participation in CPHI India 2018

It is understood that CPHI India has attracted extensive attention from manufacturers and traders of APIs all over the world, especially in China, with the vigorous development of the pharmaceutical market in India since it was first held in 2005. Focusing on the latest products and technologies in various fields such as pharmaceutical raw materials, pharmaceutical machinery and equipment, service outsourcing and biomedicine, the exhibition is dedicated to helping the industry to find high-quality and low-cost product solutions. After more than ten years of growth and success in Mumbai, CPHI has become the largest pharmaceutical exhibition in South Asia.

Figure 2: CPHI India 2018

As a first-class professional peptide drug enterprise in China, sinobio has been closely cooperating with foreign pharmaceutical peptide manufacturers in the same industry.
