Small molecule peptide: deeply reduce the side effects of radiotherapy and chemotherapy

2020-03-26

Abstract:

The main treatment of cancer

At present, cancer treatment is mainly based on "operation, chemotherapy and radiotherapy". Operation is the first choice for cancer treatment. Unfortunately, many patients have found cancer at an advanced stage and have lost the opportunity of surgery. They can only choose radiotherapy and chemotherapy. However, chemotherapy has a lot of toxic and side effects. Many patients will suffer from different side effects after operation Suffering.

Side effects of radiotherapy and chemotherapy

Decreased immune function: chemotherapy drugs can lead to the decrease of white blood cells, damage the immune system of patients, and lead to the defect or decline of immune function.

Physical weakness: chemotherapy patients will appear fatigue, mental weakness, sweating, drowsiness and other adverse reactions.

Inflammatory reaction: fever, dizziness, headache, dry mouth, sore mouth and tongue, etc. May also cause phlebitis, cystitis and other inflammatory diseases. It can also cause hair loss.

Organ toxicity: some chemotherapy drugs can cause renal function, heart function, liver function damage. Lead to organ failure and damage.

Dyspepsia: many chemotherapy drugs can cause loss of appetite, decreased diet, nausea, vomiting, abdominal distention, abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation by stimulating gastrointestinal mucosa.

Half of the patients died because of the side effects of radiotherapy and chemotherapy

Nowadays, the main treatment for cancer patients is radiotherapy and chemotherapy. But not everyone can afford the side effects. Half of the patients died because of the damage of many organs or the sharp decline of resistance caused by radiotherapy and chemotherapy. This also makes the majority of doctors and patients realize that when using radiotherapy and chemotherapy to treat cancer, we should also take a series of effective measures to relieve the side effects of chemotherapy on human body, so as to prolong the life of patients.

How to alleviate the side effects of radiotherapy and chemotherapy

In order to reduce the side effects of radiotherapy and chemotherapy, many people have used a variety of traditional Chinese medicine, health products and even food therapy. The following is a summary of the effective mitigation methods proposed by many scientific experts.

1、 Combined with professional means of anti radiotherapy and chemotherapy

  1. Change the route of administration

In some tumors, in particular cases, intrathoracic and intraperitoneal administration, arterial infusion, intratumoral injection, limb cardiopulmonary bypass infusion or "half body chemotherapy" to block the abdominal aorta can be used to improve the local drug concentration of the tumor, reduce the blood concentration of the systemic circulation system, and reduce the systemic toxicity of chemotherapy drugs.

  1. Component transfusion

In cancer chemotherapy, hematologic toxic leucopenia and thrombocytopenia are the most common, which can lead to infection and bleeding in severe cases. If necessary, transfusion of concentrated leucocyte or platelet component blood can prevent and treat serious infection and bleeding.

  1. Bone marrow transplantation

Peripheral blood stem cell transplantation (PBSCT) is usually used in solid tumor chemotherapy, such as autogenous bone alkali transplantation and PBSCT. It is to extract the patient's own bone marrow for cryopreservation before intensive chemotherapy or collect the patient's own peripheral blood stem cells for cryopreservation after mobilization, and then return them to oil after chemotherapy, which can protect some hematopoietic stem cells from the damage of anticancer drugs, and is beneficial to hematopoietic function The recovery of energy.

2、 Scientific and reasonable diet

People with radiotherapy and chemotherapy should eat more foods rich in protein, iron and vitamins, such as fish and lean meat. In addition, animal liver, jujube, longan, donkey hide gelatin, fresh fruits and vegetables are also beneficial to patients. Patients with anorexia, dyspepsia and diarrhea can be supplemented by spleen strengthening and stomach nourishing foods, such as job's tears, white lentils, dates, etc.; at the same time, it is necessary to pay attention to the change of the patient's recipes, change the cooking methods and types of dishes regularly, pay attention to the deployment of color, fragrance and taste, so as to stimulate the patients' appetite, and follow some principles in the diet.

Dietary principles:

  1. Eat less and eat more. Avoid empty stomach or abdominal distention.

  2. Take a moderate rest after dinner, but don't lie on your back.

  3. Eating dry food before and after getting up and before exercise, such as biscuits or toast, can inhibit nausea; do not eat immediately after exercise.

  4. Eating should be avoided within 2 hours before receiving radiation or chemotherapy to prevent vomiting.

  5. If the patient is not ill to the state of bedridden, before eating, the patient can be asked to do appropriate exercise, and then eat some small amount of appetizers, such as dandruff, hawthorn tablets, and finally eat the main food. Appropriate exercise will promote the metabolism of new and old, promote gastrointestinal peristalsis and increase appetite.
