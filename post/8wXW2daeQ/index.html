Anti-aging and small molecule active peptide

2020-03-10 4 min read # Peptide industry news

Aging is not only a direct manifestation of the slowing down of human function, but also a natural process. The theory of biomolecular cross-linking demonstrates the molecular mechanism of organism aging. It is pointed out that organism is an unstable chemical system, in which various biomolecules have a large number of active groups, and they must interact with each other to produce chemical reactions to slow the cross-linking of biomolecules to tend to the stability of chemical activity. With the passage of time, the active groups of biomolecules are consumed less and less, and the original molecular structure is gradually changed. The accumulation of these changes will lead to the gradual aging of biological tissues. Aging is a direct manifestation of the slowing down of human function and a natural process!

There are three stages of human aging:
The first stage: slight aging from 25 to 35 years old, mainly including color spots, decreased immunity, inattention and slight wrinkles;

The second stage: moderate aging 35-45 years old insomnia, suspicious, dry skin, decreased elasticity, rough pores, deeper wrinkles.

The third stage: serious aging 45-55 years old human body into the rapid aging period, the overall aging of the body, skin water loss wrinkling, women into the rapid aging period.

Harman first proposed in 1956 that free radicals are related to aging and diseases. Aging is a continuous process of change. The international mainstream thinks that free radicals damage human cells, cause many diseases and accelerate human aging. From generation to quenching, free radicals deeply affect the formation of lipofuscin, mutation of mitochondrial DNA, induction of apoptosis and protein synthesis, the relationship between free radicals and aging, diseases caused by free radicals, so as to put forward how to delay aging and provide a certain scientific basis for human beings to slow down their own aging.

Small molecule active peptide - peptide is a kind of biochemical substance between amino acid and protein. It is smaller than protein molecular weight and larger than amino acid molecular weight. It is a fragment of protein.

It can directly involve in the metabolism of blood cells, brain and nerve cells, muscle cells, reproductive cells, endocrine cells, skin cells, and participate in the growth, development, physiological function, division and proliferation of cells. Small molecular peptides participate in the synthesis of enzyme, stimulate the activity of enzyme, strengthen the function of enzyme and maintain the stability of enzyme. It is an important embodiment of its biological activity that small peptide enters the cell directly in its prototype and scavenges free radicals.
