Ten physiological functions of peptides

2019-12-24

Recently, Zou Yuandong, vice president of China Invention Association, vice president of China Health Care Association, world famous peptide scientist and chairman of Wuhan jiushengtang Bioengineering Co., Ltd., pointed out that protein degradation and artificial synthesis are small peptides with 2-6 amino acid chains and molecular weight less than 10000, which have ten major physiological functions.

One is carrier function. Small peptides show carrier function with their own physiological activity. It can be taken by the body of other nutrients. Such as calcium, iron, zinc, manganese, copper, potassium, sodium, various vitamins, biotin, contained in their own body.

The second is chelating function. Small peptide can chelate with various major and trace elements, chelate synthesis of small peptide binding calcium, small peptide binding zinc, small peptide binding iron, small peptide binding copper, small peptide binding manganese, etc. These major and trace elements combined with small peptides can be 100% absorbed and utilized by human body. Therefore, the supplement of calcium, iron and zinc, without peptide, is equal to white supplement. Not combined with small peptides, major and trace elements are easy to precipitate in the digestive system of the human body, and finally excluded from the body.

The third is adsorption function. After the small peptide enters the human body, it will absorb other nutrients taken by the human body on its own body.

Fourth, transportation function. After the small peptide enters the human body, it shows the function of transportation. It transports all kinds of nutrients to the parts needed by human body through the function of loading and adsorption.

Fifth, power function. After being absorbed by human body, small peptides take their own activities as motive force and participate in human life and physiological activities.

Sixth, transmitter function. After being absorbed by human body, small peptide, as a neurotransmitter, transmits information to all organs and systems of human body, making human body more intelligent, more sensitive and more tacit.

Seven is the function of "police". Each peptide has a different division of labor. Some peptides, as "police officers", will kiss her if they find the peptide that has changed in the body and is unqualified. The kissed peptide will be crushed by another kind of peptide like a "Grinder" and then discharged from the body.

Eight is the balance function. Some people think that the degradation of protein, synthetic peptides, eat too much will not be harmful, or excessive nutrition, excessive activity. The answer is yes, No. Peptide in human body, just like water in bottle, water is full and self flowing out of the body. Moreover, the speed of small peptide metabolism in human body is very fast, and the speed of human body's taking quantity is not as fast as human body's metabolism.

Nine is energy function. Protein is the main substance of human body. Human protein is achieved by absorbing small peptides. The energy of human body is the same. The heat, strength, explosive force and endurance of human body are all reactions of energy. The amount of energy saved by the human body is the amount of peptide. The higher the peptide content in human body, the stronger the activity and the greater the energy.

Antibody function. After the small peptide enters the human body, it first merges with the human cell membrane to make the cell membrane produce antibody. The antibody produced after the small peptide merges with the cell membrane prevents all kinds of viruses from entering the cell membrane, so that the cell membrane will not be infected and the human body is not easy to get sick.
