All the active substances in human body are in the form of peptides. There are many kinds of peptides in human body, involving hormone, nerve, cell growth, reproduction and other fields, which lead the growth, development, reproduction, metabolism and behavior of human body. It can promote the metabolism of cells and repair the diseased cells of human body. Peptide is also directly related to immune function, which is an important active substance for the body to complete immune function and carry out immune regulation.
