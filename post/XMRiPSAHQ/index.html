rheumatism

Rheumatism is not an independent disease, but a general term of a class of diseases.

The involved parts are: bone, joint and surrounding soft tissue, including muscle, bursa, tendon, fascia, nerve, etc.

Symptoms: pain, sleepiness, numbness, rigidity, swelling, deformation, inconvenience of movement, etc. Serious even affect the internal organs, so that the five viscera and six viscera damaged.

There are many causes: streptococcal infection, including genetic, immune disorders, metabolic disorders, endocrine disorders, degenerative changes, malignant tumor, etc.

In fact, joint pain and discomfort can be considered as rheumatism in traditional Chinese medicine.

Rheumatic diseases: rheumatoid arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, systemic lupus erythematosus, Sjogren's syndrome, gout, etc.

Rheumatoid arthritis

From the above, we can see that rheumatoid arthritis is actually one of the rheumatic diseases.

Therefore, rheumatism and rheumatoid are not the same thing. Rheumatoid is a disease with more independent and distinct occurrence site and symptoms.

The most typical manifestation of rheumatoid arthritis is synovial inflammation of joints, which can start from a small joint and gradually involve all joints of the whole body, with systemic changes and joint deformities. This disease is a systemic chronic disease, the cause of which is unknown, and the disease is stubborn and hard to cure. It is very destructive to the joint. In the late stage, there will be joint deformity, which will affect the quality of life of the patients.
