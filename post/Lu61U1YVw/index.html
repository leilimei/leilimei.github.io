The concept and development of bioactive peptides

2020-04-20

Abstract:
Peptide is a kind of compound whose molecular structure is between amino acid and protein. It is composed of two or more amino acid molecules connected with each other through peptide bond. It is the structure and functional segment of protein, and makes protein have tens of millions of physiological functions. It also has strong biological activity. Amino acid, as the basic component unit of peptide, its type, number and arrangement are in order The different order determines the complex structure and function of peptides. Peptides formed by dehydration and condensation of two or three amino acids are called dipeptides or tripeptides, and so on. There is no strict distinction between peptide and protein. It is generally believed that the number of amino acids in peptide chain is more than 10, 10-50 are oligopeptides, and more than 50 are proteins. Oligopeptides are also known as short peptides or oligopeptides, among which two or three peptides are also called small peptides.

There are natural peptide molecules in the body, which are indispensable for the normal life activities of the body. These peptide molecules involved in the physiological activities of the body are called bioactive peptides. Bioactive peptides are composed of amino acids in different arrangements, from dipeptides to complex linear and circular structures of different peptides. These peptides can be activated by phosphorylation and glycosylation.

At present, a variety of bioactive peptides have been isolated from animals, plants and microorganisms by means of in vitro enzymolysis. Their physiological functions mainly include morphine like activity, hormone and regulatory hormone. They have regulatory and inhibitory functions on enzymes in vivo, such as immune regulation, promoting growth and development, antithrombotic, antihypertensive, cholesterol lowering and fine inhibition Bacteria, virus, anti-cancer, anti-oxidation and free radical scavenging, improve element absorption and mineral transport, promote growth, etc. Bioactive peptides have good physical and chemical properties and good hydration, which make their solubility increase, viscosity decrease, gelation decrease and foaming decrease, so that their processing properties are good. Bioactive peptide is a natural resource for screening drugs, preparing vaccines and functional foods. At present, the world is competing to research and develop peptide drugs and functional food.
