In 2017, the total output of China's peptide industry was 21.92 million tons, and the sales volume was 18.783 million tons.

The peptide industry is growing rapidly, the company is small, and the growth opportunities are big. Especially some small domestic enterprises can make breakthroughs in generic drugs, import substitutes, and use the accumulated capital, technology, channels and other resources to further imitate and develop new drugs. With the rapid development of biotechnology, peptide has become another important field of life science research after gene. At present, peptide products have been widely used in medicine, food, health products, cosmetics, biomaterials, biopesticides and many other fields. Peptide drugs in China are still dominated by foreign brands, and foreign products occupy a large market share in the peptide drug market in China. The peptide drugs made in China are all the ones that have passed the patent protection period or have not applied for patent in China. Most of the chemical synthetic peptide drugs in developed countries are still in the patent protection period. The patent protection period of these peptide drugs will expire in the next 5 to 10 years, which will usher in new market opportunities for peptide generic drugs.

Peptides have many biological functions, such as antibacterial, antitumor, enhancing the activity of traditional antibiotics to resistant bacteria, promoting wound healing and so on. It has the characteristics of strong antibacterial activity, broad antibacterial spectrum, and bacteria are not easy to produce tolerance, which indicates that it has a broad application prospect in the fields of medicine, food antisepsis, health products and cosmetics.
