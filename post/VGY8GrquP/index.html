AIDS, which can damage the human immune system and cause color change, has nearly one million cases in China. At this time last year, the editor introduced the basic information about AIDS. Today, we want to talk about the current situation of AIDS patients in China.

Status of AIDS patients: discriminated

This group of people because of AIDS, work and life everywhere.

In October this year, a man was "tested" for HIV (i.e. HIV) without knowledge, and was rejected by the company; in 2015, an elderly AIDS patient was rejected when he wanted to stay in a residential care home; in 2014, the airline forced to delete three passenger information, only because there were AIDS patients, and therefore delayed the trip.

In society, such things have been common, because discrimination against some people even life is difficult, different eyes everywhere, people can not avoid.

Thinking about the current situation of AIDS

Although aids can destroy the human immune system, many people may not know that AIDS is not as terrible as they think.

First of all, AIDS transmission needs to have certain conditions (i.e. sexual contact, blood, mother to child transmission), without which it will not be easily infected, that is to say, it will not be infected in daily life.

Second, with the current medical technology, HIV can be controlled, so no matter there are patients around, or they are patients, they do not need to be too alarmed and afraid, but always keep their heads and do not avoid medical treatment.

Especially for young students, even though in recent years, all departments in China have increased efforts to implement AIDS prevention and control work in Colleges and universities, as well as AIDS knowledge publicity, many young students are still infected.

The patient's heart is already sensitive. Once someone is infected with HIV, the first reaction of most people is to stay away from and tell the people around them to stay away from this person. Sometimes some people will wear colored glasses to treat these patients, discriminate against them, make these people who are physically injured add more trauma, and avoid their own diseases, and dare not go to a doctor.

Because the patient avoids, conceals, an carelessness is one spreads ten, ten spreads hundred. Therefore, we should be more inclusive of patients and understand them. No one wants to spread the virus on purpose, and no one wants to catch such a disease. It's only their alienation and different vision that make their hearts more sensitive and vulnerable.

Why don't we abandon these stereotypes and listen to the voices of these patients?

Transposition thinking, if we are those who are infected, how do we expect others to think of us? Will it be hard for us to be treated differently? So can we let go of our prejudices and try to accept them so that they can maintain a peaceful life.

From the documentary to see the world's cold and warm

On December 1, 2010, Gu Changwei, together with the documentary film "together" and the new film "magic", starring Zhang Ziyi, Guo Fucheng and Jiang Wenli, called on everyone to oppose discrimination against people living with AIDS.

During the shooting, six infected people played roles or worked as staff, and worked and lived together with the crew. Three of them voluntarily remove the shelter and expose themselves to the camera. Together is a record of the work and life of three infected people and actors.

These actors set an example of anti discrimination against AIDS. They not only don't isolate them, but also talk and hug ordinary people or friends with them. It is this kind of behavior that makes those infected people more confident and courageous to face the camera and let everyone see the most real themselves.

Therefore, each of us should treat these infected people with a normal attitude, help them come out of the shadow of AIDS, let them dare to face and actively seek medical treatment.

Summary

At present, AIDS has been controllable, so it is not terrible, but what we really need to think about is: our attitude towards AIDS patients, how we guide the society to attach importance to the human rights of patients, how to promote the anti discrimination work, how to let patients get fair and just life and work opportunities.

Because only by building a harmonious and inclusive society and guiding with positive and correct values can we effectively prevent and control AIDS together and curb its prevalence. We should also vigorously popularize relevant knowledge, clean ourselves and actively promote, and call on people around us to join the ranks of AIDS anti discrimination and make contributions to the construction of a healthy China.
