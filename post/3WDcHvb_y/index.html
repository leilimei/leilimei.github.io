People with slightly higher uric acid should adjust their lifestyle

2020-03-24

Abstract:

For those who have high uric acid but don't need to take medicine temporarily after the doctor's assessment, they should pay attention to adjusting their lifestyle: "keep your mouth in check, open your legs, lose weight, drink more water, and prohibit drinking.".

Keep your mouth shut, including restricting foods high in purine and fructose. Step on your legs, that is, pay attention to exercise. Exercise is good for reducing uric acid.

Obese people often have metabolic abnormalities, more likely to suffer from gout, diabetes and other diseases, so weight loss, weight control is also beneficial.

Adequate drinking water can help excrete uric acid, but alcohol can affect the metabolism of uric acid and even induce gout.

In addition to lifestyle adjustment, friends with high uric acid should also pay attention to regular physical examination and reexamination of blood uric acid, blood sugar, blood pressure, blood lipid, etc. Only in this way can we master our own health and find out the aggravation of the disease as soon as possible.

The first is used to reduce uric acid, such as allopurinol, non bustard, benzbromarone, etc.

● second, analgesic drugs such as colchicine, non steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs and glucocorticoids.
