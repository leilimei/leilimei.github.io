Surgery is an important means to treat surgical diseases. While satisfying the treatment, it is also a kind of trauma, which leads to a series of changes in endocrine and metabolism.

●● significant increase in energy metabolism, lasting for 5-6 days.

●● protein, fat and carbohydrate catabolism increased.

●● amino acids decomposed by proteins are not only converted into glucose, but also used to synthesize new proteins, meet the needs of body repair and provide enzymes, antibodies and other substances for metabolic reactions. At this time, the body is in a negative nitrogen balance, indicating that the amount of protein synthesis in the body is less than the amount of decomposition. If the postoperative protein intake is insufficient, it will cause the body to lose weight, reduce the resistance to disease, and the wound of patients is difficult to heal.

The necessity of peptide nutrition after surgery

During the treatment of surgical patients, there are often some diseases that hinder the intake of food, or affect the normal digestion and absorption of food in the gastrointestinal tract, accompanied by malnutrition and low immune function. Surgical trauma and complications or associated infection make the body in a state of high metabolism. The inflammatory response induced by them is more harmful to immunity and nutrition, which leads to the increase of surgical complications. Peptide nutrition support can support the nutritional status of patients, maintain nitrogen balance, reduce the loss of thin body, participate in immune regulation, accelerate tissue repair, and promote the recovery of patients.

Nutritional value of bioactive peptides

\1. Comprehensive nutrition and safe eating

The amino acid composition of bioactive peptide is balanced and comprehensive, and it contains many essential amino acids. The proportion of essential amino acids is close to that of reference protein. From the perspective of food safety, the research group of Professor Li Yong from the Department of nutrition and food hygiene of Peking University evaluated the safety of bioactive peptides, which proved the safety and reliability of bioactive peptides.

Easy to absorb and fast to absorb

Compared with proteins and amino acids, bioactive peptides have more prominent advantages in the absorption and operation of the body.

●● high absorption rate: compared with protein, the molecular weight of bioactive oligopeptide is small, and it does not need to be digested and directly absorbed into the blood, so it has better absorption rate.

●● high transport speed: the absorption and metabolism speed of oligopeptides is faster than that of free amino acids, because the transport systems of the two are different, and there is no absorption competition; many bioactive peptides can be actively absorbed, preferentially absorbed, and the absorption process has no or less energy consumption; when the amino acid transport system is blocked, the absorption of oligopeptides can achieve good absorption effect. Therefore, when oligopeptide is used to provide nutrients for the body, it is beneficial to exert the function effect as soon as possible.

Improve immunity

Bioactive peptides have strong antioxidant activity, can clear the free radicals in the body, reduce the inflammatory reaction to a certain extent, play a role in improving the immunity of the body, and prevent enterogenous infection and organ failure. In addition, bioactive peptides can stimulate the immune response to physical injury and mediate the recovery of immune function.

Promote wound healing

Surgery itself is a kind of wound. It is an important task to find an effective wound healing agent. Bioactive peptides can directly penetrate into the bottom of the skin and have good affinity with surrounding tissues, which can help cells to produce collagen and promote the normal growth of skin cells. At the same time, collagen also has the function of anti-inflammatory, renewing skin and promoting the expression of growth factors.

●● effect on wound healing in general surgery

● impact on cesarean section

Cesarean section is also a kind of surgical operation. Taking small molecule bioactive peptide after delivery can provide rich nutrition for the body, accelerate wound healing, increase skin anti pulling force, make the wound surface smooth without scab, reduce the risk of placenta previa in the second pregnancy, and make the maternal body have a better improvement.

[recommended use]

It is recommended to take 8g of collagen peptide on an empty stomach every day before operation. For patients with malnutrition, it can be added to 16g to improve the nutritional level, improve the immunity of the body and the ability to cope with postoperative complications.

When you can eat after operation, it is recommended to take 8-16g of collagen peptide on an empty stomach every day to provide nutrition for the body, promote wound healing and improve immunity.
