The best beauty medicine: sleeping

It's better than expensive cosmetics.

Studies have shown that the most active time of metabolism of human epidermal cells is from 22 to 2 a.m. the next day. Experts explained that this is the best time for us to say "beauty sleep". If we can ensure good sleep during this time, we can speed up skin metabolism and delay skin aging. On the contrary, staying up late will affect the speed of cell regeneration, shorten the life of skin cells, and lead to skin aging.

Be sure to remember to go to bed before 23 o'clock.
