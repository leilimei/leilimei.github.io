About peptides, let's start with proteins!

It can be seen that peptides are the structural and functional segments of proteins, and also the ordered composition of amino acids. So peptide is a compound composed of two or more amino acid molecules connected by peptide bond. A general term for a group of substances with small molecular active proteins with molecular weight between 180 and 10000. More than 70% of human protein is absorbed in the form of peptide. In fact, most of the functional proteins in the human body are carriers, and their functions are performed by the peptide segments on them.
