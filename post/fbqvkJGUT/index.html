Ten nutritional (absorption mechanism) characteristics of peptides

2019-12-31

In the past, it was believed that protein was mainly absorbed in the form of amino acids after enzymatic hydrolysis in the digestive tract. In recent two years, scientific research has shown that the main form of protein absorption by human body is not amino acid, but polypeptide, which is a major breakthrough in the study of the mechanism of protein absorption by human body. Scientific experiments show that the absorption mechanism of peptides has ten characteristics:

  1. No digestion, direct absorption. Generally, polypeptides are synthesized by the human body itself, which is the enzymatic hydrolysis of the protein eaten by the human body (catalyzing enzyme, digestive enzyme, pancreatin, pepsin, gastric acid and alkaline substances in the digestive tract are decomposed, first into amino acids, then synthetic peptides, finally absorbed through the small intestine, and then through the large circulation of human cells, tissues, organs and blood). It has been synthesized in vitro. After entering the human body, it does not need to be degraded twice and absorbed directly

  2. The absorption is fast, and the oral dose is like an injection. The synthetic polypeptide in vitro enters the human body by oral administration at the same speed as the rocket. Some scientists call it "biological missile", which quickly passes through the human mouth and stomach, directly enters the small intestine, is absorbed by the small intestine, and finally enters the human blood circulation system, organs and cellular tissues, and rapidly plays its physiological and biological functions.

  3. Absorbed in a complete form. The polypeptide body has a protective film of its own. When taken, it will not be damaged or re hydrolyzed by the enzyme, pancreatin, amylase, digestive enzyme, pepsin and acid-base substances in the digestive system. The polypeptide is absorbed and utilized by the human body in a complete form.

  4. Peptide has the characteristics of 100% absorption by human body. After absorption, there will be no excreta, all of which are absorbed and utilized by the human body.

  5. The peptide has the characteristics of active absorption by human body. The absorption of any substance by human body consumes human energy, while the absorption of peptide does not need to consume human energy. Peptide forces human body to absorb with its own energy. For those who cannot absorb nutrients due to digestive system defects, obstacles and damages. It is of great significance for those who need to supplement nitrogen urgently, but can't increase the burden of gastrointestinal function, and for those who have poor digestion, lack of nutrition, weak body and many diseases. Peptide itself has very strong activity and energy. Its active absorption and forced absorption are its own activity and energy. Therefore, when it is absorbed by the human body, it is not that the human body needs to consume its own energy to absorb it, but the polypeptide uses its own energy to let the human body absorb it. From this point of view, this remarkable feature is of great significance to the infant with immature digestive system, the elderly with degenerative digestive system and the athletes and manual workers who are in urgent need of nitrogen source due to excessive exercise, but can not increase the burden of gastrointestinal function.

  6. Peptide has the characteristics of being absorbed by human body preferentially. People usually eat nutrients, in the absorption, and peptide competition, peptide has the characteristics of priority absorption. This is inseparable from the characteristics of active absorption.

  7. Small peptide can protect amino acids when it is absorbed by human body. It can protect amino acids from damage. Therefore, the mixture of peptide and amino acid is the best absorption mechanism of protein.

  8. Polypeptides play a carrier role in human body. It can absorb, paste and load the nutrients, especially calcium, which are beneficial to human body.

  9. Polypeptides can be used as means of transport in human body. It can adsorb all kinds of nutrients that people usually eat on the body, then transport them to all cells, organs and tissues of the human body, together with the body, it can be absorbed and utilized by the human body, and play different functions. This is the reason that peptide raw material intermediates are used as drug and food formulations in the world at present. Its purpose is to enhance the efficacy and absorption rate.

  10. After the polypeptide is absorbed by human body, it plays a role of messenger in human body. As a neurotransmitter, it can transmit information and make all systems, organs and tissues play their own and overall roles.

In a word, the absorption mechanism of peptides is quite different from that of other substances. The absorption mechanism of polypeptide is very special and unique. The absorption mechanism of peptides is superior to that of all substances. Therefore, the discovery of its absorption mechanism is of great significance to human physiological health.
