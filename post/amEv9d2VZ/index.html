1、 Peptide plays an active role in human body

All active substances in human body are in the form of peptides. If there is no peptide in the human body, there will be no activity. If there is no activity in the human body, life will stop. The human body is alive, that is, the peptide still exists, and the peptide still works. All the life activities of the human body are related to the existence of the peptide. 2、 Peptides play the role of hormones in the human body

Peptide plays the role of many hormones in human body. It is the substrate of many hormones in human body, and also the source and source of many hormones. If we lack peptide as the substrate and source of hormone, the hormone will become water without source and wood without source. The hormone will not be formed or even dried up. If the human body lacks hormone, life will stop.

3、 Peptide plays a role in promoting growth and development in human body

The growth and development of human body is related to many factors, especially human protein. Human protein exists in the form of peptide. The primary material basis of human growth and development is peptide; human growth and development cannot be separated from hormone, which is also a manifestation of peptide. Therefore, no matter from which aspect, the growth and development of human body is inseparable from peptides, which play an important role in the growth and development of human body.

4、 Peptides act as neurotransmitters in the human body

The living nerves of the human body are all in the form of peptides. Peptides play a role of neurotransmitters in the human body. They can transmit information, make the human body more agile, more flexible, make all parts and organs of the human body more consistent, and make all life activities of the human body more perfect, more coordinated, more consistent and more successful. If the human body lacks peptide and nerve activity, all life activities will be slow and paralyzed, and diseases will occur.

5、 Peptides act as carriers in human body

The carrier function of peptide in human body is mainly manifested in that it can carry all kinds of nutrients, such as calcium, iron, zinc, manganese, selenium and other major and trace elements, which are usually eaten by human body on its own body, and transport them to the parts needed by human body to play various physiological activities, so as to meet all kinds of nutrients needed by human body. All calcium, major elements, iron, zinc, manganese, selenium and other trace elements must be combined with peptides, otherwise they cannot be absorbed by the human body, will produce in-situ precipitation, and will be discharged from the body. Therefore, if calcium is not supplemented with peptides, it is equal to white calcium.

6、 Peptide plays a role as a means of transport in human body

Peptide in the human body as a means of transport, the outside into the human body, the human body already exists to transport all kinds of nutrients to the parts needed by the human body, so that all kinds of nutrients needed by the human body through the transport of peptide, smoothly to reach the parts needed by the human body, to meet the timely needs of human nutrition.

All peptides can synthesize cells after entering the human body, at the same time, they can regulate the functional activities of cells, and also affect the specific protein synthesis through DNA transcription.

When the peptide enters the human body, it first merges with the human cell membrane, and then penetrates its own genetic material into the nucleus to make the cell produce immune antibody. In this way, when a virus cell enters the human body, it also needs to fuse with the cell membrane of the human body. After 4 hours, they will automatically apoptosis, and the human body will not produce diseases or be infected by the virus.
