\1. Constitution of human body

If the human body is regarded as a building, cells are the bricks and tiles of the building, and protein is the main material for building bricks and tiles.

\2. Protein composition

The heterogenous protein provided in food can not be directly used by human body after entering the human body. It must be decomposed into 20 amino acids needed by human body before it can be reconstituted into human body's own protein. In the past, it was believed that amino acids formed proteins directly. However, it was found that amino acids could not form proteins directly, but must combine with each other to form peptide chains, and then form proteins by folding and winding peptide chains.

\3. Peptide composition

Peptide is a substance between amino acid and protein. It is a combination of two or more amino acids linked by peptide bond.

Although there are only 20 kinds of amino acids that can be absorbed and utilized by human body, hundreds of peptides can be constructed by permutation and combination of different kinds and different amounts of amino acids.
