Can the R & D capitalization rate be higher than that of peers? Can we seize the opportunity of localization of drugs with expired patents?

2020-07-10

During the reporting period, the research projects were steadily promoted. The company's bivalirude for injection was approved to be listed by the food and Drug Administration of the United States, and the "bivalirudin for injection" drug registration approval document issued by the State Drug Administration in January 2019, and the application materials for the new consistency evaluation of thymus method for injection have been submitted;

The process optimization of somatostatin API has been completed, and the research on the consistency of injection has been carried out according to the plan; anda of etibatide injection has been returned to completion, pending approval.

In 2019, the company's R & D investment was 49.9498 million yuan, up 143.87% year-on-year. The capitalized R & D investment was 36.8092 million yuan, with a year-on-year increase of 518.62%, and the proportion of capitalized R & D investment in R & D was 73.69%.

The R & D capitalization rates of Hanyu pharmaceutical, xinlitai (34.810, - 0.58, - 1.64%) and saisheng Pharmaceutical (23.580, - 0.09, - 0.38%) were 30.94%, 26.3% and 24.03%, respectively, which were far lower than Shuangcheng pharmaceutical.

In response to the inquiry letter, the company said that the main reason was that pregabalin capsule and memantine hydrochloride tablets were purchased proprietary technologies purchased by the company during the reporting period, and the purchase expenditure of this period was 18113200 yuan, which led to a significant increase in the capitalization amount of R & D expenditure.

Although the peptide drug market in China started late, it has developed rapidly. The sales volume increased from 5.6 billion yuan in 2009 to 29.56 billion yuan in 2017, with an average annual compound growth rate of 23.12%. As a whole, China's polypeptide drug market is still in the development stage. Compared with foreign peptide pharmaceutical companies Novartis, Huiling pharmaceutical, prebiotics, syranox, AstraZeneca and Lilly, the R & D strength of Chinese peptide pharmaceutical enterprises is weak, and the product pipeline is relatively single.

However, in the field of peptide generic drugs, many polypeptide pharmaceutical enterprises in China have shown competitiveness, and have successively realized the localization of bivalirudin, ganerik, sittrek, enfoviride, carbetoxine, atosiban acetate and etibatide.

At present, nearly half of the peptide drugs on the market in the world have not yet been listed in China. It is certain that in the future, China's polypeptide drug industry will still be dominated by generic drugs. 2014-2022 is the peak period of patent expiration of polypeptide drugs heavyweight varieties. Among them, there are many global sales of more than US $1 billion such as gratirale, liraglutide, exenatide, goserelin, etc. In the R & D pipeline of Shuangcheng pharmaceutical industry, liraglutide and exenatide also have a layout.

Whether or not to seize the market opportunity of generic drugs after the expiration of patent is worthy of continuous attention. (article / flying mouse of sina finance and economics listed company research institute)
