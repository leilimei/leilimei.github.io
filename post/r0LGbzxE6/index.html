Peptide drugs usually refer to peptide hormones. Generally, compounds composed of amino acid residues below 50 or 50 are included in polypeptides. It is known that there are many hormones and active polypeptides in the body, and there are nearly 40 kinds in the brain alone, and people are still discovering, separating and purifying new active polypeptides. The concentration of polypeptide in the blood is very low, generally 10-10-mol / L, but its physiological activity is very strong, which plays a very important role in regulating physiological function. According to the function and secretion site of polypeptide drugs, it can be divided into:

  1. Vasopressin and its derivatives

There are neurohypophysin, vasopressin, tannic acid vasopressin, deaminamine vasopressin, benzodiazepine, guanvasopressin (POR-8), etc.

  1. Oxytocin and its derivatives

There are oxytocin, deamino oxytocin, oxytocin tartaric acid, etc

  1. Corticotropin and its derivatives

There are corticotropin, zinc corticotropin (ACTH Zn), phosphorus zinc corticotropin, gelatin corticotropin, Carboxycellulose corticotropin, sericin 18 peptide, glycine 18 peptide, zinc 24 peptide, corticotropin 24 peptide, 25 peptide, 28 peptide, etc.

  1. Hypothalamic pituitary peptide hormone

There are gonadotropin releasing hormone, thyrotropin releasing hormone, growth hormone releasing hormone (chrh), growth hormone inhibiting hormone (Chih), melanocyte inhibiting hormone (mrih), melanocyte releasing hormone (MRH), prolactin releasing hormone (PRH), prolactin inhibiting hormone (PIH), corticotropin releasing hormone (LRH), etc.

  1. Gastrointestinal hormones

There are gastrin 34 peptide, 17 peptide, 14 peptide, 5 peptide, 4 peptide, pancreatin, cholecystokinin 39 peptide, 33 peptide, 8 peptide, gastric inhibitory peptide, motilin, vasoactive peptide, pancreatic peptide, substance P, neurodepressor peptide, frog skin peptide 14 peptide, 10 peptide, etc.

  1. Other hormones and active peptides

There are thymosin, glucagon, calcitonin, angiotension I (10 peptide), II (8 peptide), III (7 peptide), enkephalin, endorphin, sleep peptide, memory peptide, pineal peptide, trypsin inhibitor, etc
